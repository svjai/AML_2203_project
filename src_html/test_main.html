2 Days to Go - Feb 15th Co-op Job Fair

by Coop and Career Services @Lambton College in Toronto - Tuesday, 13 February 2024, 11:05 AM



Lambton College in Toronto Co-op Job Fair

Date: February 15, 2024

Check-in Time: 10:45 AM

Event Time: 11:30 AM to 01:30 PM

Location: Atrium Area, 271 Yorkland Blvd, Toronto, ON M2J 1S5

 

Please continue to check myCareer Co-op Event page for the updated participant companies and their pre-approved Co-op postings these two days! It’s a good idea to print your documents, such as resumes, in advance to avoid last-minute lineups at the campus printers.

 

We are limiting the atrium access to Summer Co-op students only. Upon arrival, please check in and collect your WRISTBAND from the designated study area, just a few steps away from the atrium. Check-in starts at 10:45 AM, Thursday, February 15th.

 

Additionally, there will be 20 student volunteers assisting our Corporate Relations Team with check-ins and crowd control. If you require assistance or have questions during the Co-op Job Fair, please do not hesitate to speak with one of our volunteers! Our volunteers are in black tops with a name tag. 

 

We look forward to meeting you at our Co-op Job Fair this Thursday!  

 

All the best!

Thank you,

Register now -Understanding Ontario’s Personal Income Tax, Credits and Benefits for International Students

by Ash Rai - Friday, 9 February 2024, 9:31 AM

As the tax session is approaching COSTI’s International Student Connect (ISC) and Orientation to Ontario (O2O) programs in collaboration with the Ontario Ministry of Finance and Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) would like to invite you to attend a free, one hour webinar designed to help international and domestic students understand personal income tax, credits, and benefits.

The webinar will be conducted by Jeremy Bertrand, Senior Program Advisory Specialist in the Ontario Ministry of Finance and Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

There will also be representatives from CRA to answer questions. We are offering this webinar on eight different dates and times to be able to accommodate everyone. There is one French session too.

All staff are welcome to attend these webinars as they present an excellent opportunity for professional development. Please share the links below or distribute the attached flyer with your clients (international and domestic students) and your network.
